How does the Airbnb
calculator work?

When you enter an address, Rentalizer compiles a list of
nearby properties and creates an index based on
relevance. The more similar a property, the more weight
it has on our calculation.
Rentalizer also factors in market-wide vacation rental
metrics such as seasonality, rental demand, and revenue
growth.
By comparing similar rental properties, our Airbnb
estimator makes extremely accurate assumptions on
the earning potential for any address, anywhere in the
world. As a result, you know how much a property could
earn if listed as a full-time short-term rental.
